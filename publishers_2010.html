    Here are some interesting facts about the video game industry during this time!

  • Xbox Kinect, a device which tracks and uses human movements as a game input, was released in 2010. The kinect game was extremely popular!
  • In 2011 Nintendo released their 3DS, which was similar to the original 2DS but included 3D technology. A handful of popular Pokemon and Mario games would follow!
  • From 2011-2015, Activision released a series of Call of Duty games that were massive hits! These games are still popular today!
  • 2013 was the released of Grand Theft Auto 5, a very popular game today and still waiting for its predecessor, Grand Theft Auto 6
