Video Games from 1980-1989

"Nintendo pioneers the way for home gaming"

Here are some interesting facts about the video game industry during this time!

  • The Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) was released in 1983 and revolutionized the gaming industry with iconic games like Super Mario Bros.
  • Tetris, a puzzle game developed by Alexey Pajitnov, was released in 1984 and became one of the best-selling video games of all time.
  • The Sega Master System was released in 1985 as a direct competitor to the NES, featuring popular games like Sonic the Hedgehog.
  • In 1989, Nintendo released the Game Boy, the first handheld gaming console, along with the game Tetris, which became a massive hit.
