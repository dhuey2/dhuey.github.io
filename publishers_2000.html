Video Games from 2000-2009

"Nintendo's golden era and the age of handheld gaming"

Here are some interesting facts about the video game industry during this time!

  • The Nintendo Wii, released in 2006, introduced motion controls and became a huge success with games like Wii Sports and The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess.
  • Of the top 10 games best selling games of all time, 6 of them fall into this time period with all of them being produced by Nintendo!
  • Sony's PlayStation 2, released in 2000, became the best-selling console of all time, with popular games like Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas and Final Fantasy X.
  • Microsoft entered the gaming industry with the release of the Xbox in 2001, featuring the hit game Halo: Combat Evolved.
  • In 2004, Nintendo released the Nintendo DS, a handheld console with dual screens and touch controls, along with games like Mario Kart DS and Nintendogs.
