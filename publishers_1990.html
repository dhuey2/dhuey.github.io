Video Games from 1990-1999

Below is a graph and table related to video games sales in 1990-1999. Here are some interesting facts about the video game industry during this time!

"Nintendo continues to pave the way for modern gaming"

  • The Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) was released in 1990, featuring legendary games like The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past and Super Mario World.
  • In 1994, Sony entered the gaming market with the PlayStation, which became a major success with games like Final Fantasy VII and Gran Turismo.
  • The Pokémon franchise began in 1996 with the release of Pokémon Red and Green in Japan, quickly becoming a global phenomenon.
  • Nintendo released the Nintendo 64 in 1996, introducing 3D gaming with titles like Super Mario 64 and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time.
